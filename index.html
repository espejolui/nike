NIKE 2,600 METERS
CLOSER TO THE STARS

August 5th, 2025 – The opening of Nike's most important store in Colombia marks a major milestone in the brand's expansion across the country. Located in Bogotá—a city that sits 2,600 meters above sea level, vibrant, passionate, diverse, and perfectly chaotic—the store reopens on the very same street as before, now in a larger, more immersive space designed to elevate the consumer experience.

With a renewed focus on service, innovation, and community, this new location is built to serve athletes of all ages and inspire movement at every level.

Nike Rise Calle 82 was designed as the central hub for sport and lifestyle across Colombia, Venezuela, and Central America. Spanning three floors and 1,686 square meters, it stands as the largest Nike Rise in the region.

The store delivers a pinnacle Nike experience, seamlessly combining the best Performance and Lifestyle assortment, while introducing the first Jordan Destination space and a hyperlocal Nike By You execution. It also features an outstanding rooftop with an incredible view dedicated to premium, elevated consumer experiences, bringing the total footprint to an impressive 2,000 square meters.

We were intentional about making this space unique—obsessing over product storytelling, visual merchandising, and styling at every touchpoint to create a retail experience that feels thoughtful, elevated, and deeply connected to our consumer.

NIKE BOGOTA
BRAND IDENTITY

With a hyperlocal approach rooted in the energy, culture, and everyday life of Bogotá, we developed a visual identity for Nike Bogota that truly connects with the city.

Inspired by Bogotá's iconic landmarks, its vibrant street art, and the feeling of a city in constant construction, this visual expression embraces a raw, sketchy aesthetic that reflects the city's evolving spirit—creating a design language that locals can see, recognize, and feel as their own.

CELEBRATE THE CITY THROUGH NBY x BOGOTÁ

Get familiar with some local slang! In Bogotá, people aren’t just bogotanos—they’re proudly known as **rolos** and **rolas**. This is the city where Colombians from all regions come to chase their dreams, and honestly, ¡eso es una **chimba**! (that's so cool!). Here, everyone's your **vecí**—a friendly way to greet someone, even if you’ve never met.

Ask a rolo about the one landmark they’d save if a meteor were heading for Bogotá, and the answer comes without hesitation: Monserrate. Towering over the city at more than 3,000 meters above sea level, this iconic mountain is more than just a viewpoint—it’s a spiritual symbol, a cultural touchstone, and a place where tradition and the city’s soul
