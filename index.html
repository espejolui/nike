NIKE 2,600 METERS
CLOSER TO THE STARS

August 5th, 2025 – The opening of Nike's most important store in Colombia marks a major milestone in the brand's expansion across the country. Located in Bogotá—a city that sits 2,600 meters above sea level, vibrant, passionate, diverse, and perfectly chaotic—the store reopens on the very same street as before, now in a larger, more immersive space designed to elevate the consumer experience.

With a renewed focus on service, innovation, and community, this new location is built to serve athletes of all ages and inspire movement at every level.
