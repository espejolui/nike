Get familiar with some local slang! In Bogotá, people aren’t just bogotanos—they’re proudly known as **rolos** and **rolas**. This is the city where Colombians from all regions come to chase their dreams, and honestly, ¡eso es una **chimba**! (that's so cool!). Here, everyone's your **vecí**—a friendly way to greet someone, even if you’ve never met.

Ask a rolo about the one landmark they’d save if a meteor were heading for Bogotá, and the answer comes without hesitation: Monserrate.
